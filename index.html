Who We Are ...

Zenith is a scientific club dedicated to connect medicine and technology, empowering students and professionals to innovate and make a tangible difference in healthcare through knowledge, creativity, and collaboration.

The Deep Learning Indaba is a grassroots organization uniting African AI researchers and practitioners to strengthen artificial intelligence and machine learning in Africa, empowering communities to actively shape and own the future of AI.
